Summary of Research Activities

The activities carried out during my research career are in the general field of digital communication systems with particular attention to those in wireless environments. Scientific contributions are transversal to different types of systems, both traditional (point-to-point) and more advanced networking scenarios (with multiple users and ad-hoc communications).

  • Transmission and distributed detection in sensor networks

    • decentralized detection of physical phenomena

    • modeling and applications to IEEE 802.15.4 systems

    • clustered vehicular networks

  • Signal processing for sensor networks

    • efficient in-sensor signal processing

  • Transmission systems on non-ideal channels

    • low-complexity channel estimation in cellular networks

    • communications over the phase noise channel

    • communications of correlated sources over fading channels

  • Advanced applications for communication networks

    • high-accuracy localization in the Internet of Things (IoT)

    • heterogeneous networks and cognitive networking

    • network coding for fast data dissemination and storage

  • Audio signal processing

    • control of the acoustic field with applications to automotive scenarios
